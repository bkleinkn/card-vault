About Card Vault

Card Vault helps you figure out what your sports cards are and roughly what they may be worth. Take a photo, the app identifies the card, and you can save it to a personal collection.

How to scan well

  • Lay the card on a flat, non-shiny surface.
  • Use good light — natural daylight is best.
  • Fill the frame with the card.
  • Make sure the player's name and the year are easy to read.

About the value estimate

The dollar range is a rough ballpark. It does not know the exact condition of your card and it does not see live market prices.

Before buying or selling, always confirm with recent eBay "sold" listings. On eBay, search the card (for example, "1956 Topps Mickey Mantle 135") and filter to Sold Items.

When to call a professional

For any single card that might be worth $500 or more, get a second opinion before selling. Reputable card-grading companies like PSA, SGC, and BGS will give you an official condition grade and a more reliable value.
